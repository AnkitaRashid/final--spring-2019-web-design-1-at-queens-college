Here’s a brief history of who I am, what I do, and why we should be friends. I was born and raised in a traditional family in Bangladesh. When I was 18, I decided to fly half way across the world to New York City to break cultural barriers, expectations and pursue a seemingly “impractical career”. I ended up attending Queens College and falling in love with every artistic disciple during my junior year. I declared Graphic Design as my minor when I found myself doodling notes instead of writing them. Primarily, I am an illustrator and visual designer with developing knowledge on editorial and package design. Over the past 2 years, I’ve explored plenty of creative software tools to channel my creative thought process; and I hope this portfolio sheds a light on my graphic design aesthetic.
